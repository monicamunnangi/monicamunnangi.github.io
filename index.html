I am graduate student UMass Amherst, where I work on computer vision and machine learning. I am a Graduate Student Researcher at Information Fusion Lab advised by Prof. Madalina Fiterau working on an end to end Alzheimers progession model FLARe.

Last summer I interned at GE Healthcare as a Data Scientist. In my time there I worked on a classifier to detect the presence of chest tubes in X-Ray images. I had the opportunity to work with Katelyn Nye and got hands on experience with TensoFlow and Keras. The abstract was accepted at the European Congress of Radiology which is to be held in March 2020 at Vienna.

Prior to UMass Amherst I've done my undergrad at Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai where I was introduced to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and was advised by Prof. Priyadarshini J.
